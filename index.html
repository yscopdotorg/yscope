Our Purposes

What we Provide

Health Awareness
Community Research
Literacy Campaign
Economic Support

  • Health Awareness

    In South Asia, more than 30% of women and girls still struggle to manage their menstruation hygienically due to lack of water, soap, and privacy. This is not just a matter of hygiene— it’s about dignity, health, and equal opportunity.

    When girls cannot manage their periods safely, they often miss school, face health risks, and endure social stigma

  • Community Research

    Lower-income group people in Bangladesh face numerous challenges that impact their daily lives. We have visited the lower income people at Mazargate area of Oxygen, Chattogram to acknowledge their daily life and their challenges.

    They face a multitude of challenges including poverty, limited access to healthcare and education, inadequate housing, and limited employment.

  • Literacy campaign

    Our team visited Nazirar Tek and engaged with the local fishing community.

    We will work alongside these fishermen to provide foundational literacy support, including basic reading and writing skills. We believe that strengthening their literacy capacity will enhance their daily communication and create new opportunities for personal & economic advancement.

  • Economic Empowerment

    "Economic freedom is the foundation of women’s dignity."

    We worked on assessing and improving the economic conditions of women affected by floods. Our vision is to empower these women to build sustainable economic prosperity within their communities. They aspire to use their hard-earned savings to support their children’s education and secure a better future for their families.
